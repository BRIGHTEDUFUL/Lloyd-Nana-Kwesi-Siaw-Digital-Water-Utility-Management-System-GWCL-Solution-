Overview

This solution digitizes Ghana Water Company Limited (GWCL) operations with an integrated platform for customer onboarding, smart metering, billing, collections, service requests, asset management, and performance analytics. Built for scalability, transparency, and better customer experience.

Key Features

  • Unified customer 360 and account lifecycle management
  • Smart/AMR meter integration and remote reads
  • Automated billing, tariff, and arrears workflows
  • Digital payments with reconciliation and audit trails
  • Work orders, service requests, and field operations
  • Dashboards, KPIs, leak detection, and revenue protection

Customer & Billing

Onboarding, account changes, billing runs, notices, and collections.

Metering

Smart meter integration, anomaly detection, and estimated reads.

Operations

Work orders, asset registry, maintenance scheduling, and field apps.

Analytics

Performance dashboards, NRW insights, demand forecasts, and compliance.

Deployment

The website is served via GitHub Pages from the main branch. Add further documentation, API specs, and demos under the assets/ folder. CI/CD can be integrated to publish updates automatically.
